## 💡 KEY INSIGHTS FROM BUILD

### What Works (Keep Doing)
1. **Research first** → Market analysis saved weeks of wrong decisions
2. **Psychology-backed** → Gamification, social proof, scarcity all tested
3. **Trinidad-specific** → COD, local banks, WhatsApp = huge differentiators
4. **Autonomous ops** → AI agents enable 1-person operation at scale
5. **Community-driven** → Network effects = cheapest customer acquisition

### What to Watch
1. **Payment verification** → Manual bank transfers may create support load
2. **COD fraud** → Need fraud detection on high-value COD orders
3. **Template quality** → User expectations will increase over time
4. **Competition** → Shopify/Wix may copy our Trinidad-specific features
5. **Scale costs** → Transaction volume may require infrastructure upgrades
