## 📈 COLD TRAFFIC LANDING PAGE - CRO CHECKLIST

### Page Structure (Proven for SaaS)

```
✅ HERO SECTION (Above Fold)
   - Eyebrow: "🇹🇹 Built for Trinidad & Tobago"
   - Headline: "Sell Online with COD — No Credit Card"
   - Subheadline: "5-minute setup. 10 free products. 5,000+ merchants."
   - Primary CTA: "Start My Free Store" (high contrast, 45px button)
   - Trust strip: Free / 10 listings / No CC / Fast setup
   - Parallax/animation (but lightweight for mobile)

✅ SOCIAL PROOF (Trust)
   - 4 stat cards: "5,000+ Businesses", "TT$2.5M Orders", "50K+ Products", "4.9/5 Rating"
   - 4 merchant stories with emoji + quote + outcome
   - "Real results from real T&T merchants"

✅ HOW IT WORKS (Clarity)
   - 4-step visual flow (no text walls)
   - Step 1: Create → Step 2: Add Products → Step 3: Get Orders → Step 4: Collect Cash
   - Each step shows emoji + 1-line outcome

✅ COD EXPLAINED (Remove Objection)
   - "Here's exactly how cash on delivery works"
   - 4 sequential steps: Order Placed → You Notified → Confirm Delivery → Order Complete
   - Each with specific outcome (no vague promises)

✅ FEATURES (Packaged Value)
   - 8 core features (not 20+) grouped logically
   - Free Store / Products / COD / AI Lister / Dashboard / VAT / Delivery / WhatsApp
   - Each has icon + headline + 1-line description

✅ PRICING (Revenue)
   - 3 tiers: Free (forever) / Pro (TT$199) / Business (TT$399)
   - Pro highlighted (highest revenue tier)
   - Feature comparison minimal but clear
   - "All plans include..." disclaimer

✅ FAQ / OBJECTIONS (Reduce Friction)
   - 5 critical questions (not 15):
     • Do I need credit card? (No)
     • What if customer doesn't answer? (Your control)
     • How fast to first order? (Days, not weeks)
     • Do I need inventory system? (No)
     • Will customers trust me? (Yes - verified badge)

✅ FINAL CTA (Confirmation)
   - Headline: "Ready to Start Selling?"
   - Subheadline: "First 10 listings free. No risk."
   - CTA: "Create My Free Store" (same button style as hero)
   - Copy clarity over creativity

✅ FOOTER CTA (Safety Net)
   - "Still unsure? Chat with us"
   - WhatsApp link (primary)
   - Message form (secondary)
```
